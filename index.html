About us

What we do

The problem we are trying to solve is unemployment. And in most cases when we are talking about being employed all we think about is working for a certain company or person. So we came up with an idea to build a website that will educate unemployed people about being an entrepreneur and empower them to build their own companies or sites. On this website we are trying to make the rate of unemployment decrease. This website will have an educate section where people will be able to learn about how to become an entrepreneur. And there will also be a funder section where you will get information about the funder so that you can apply for business to get funding.

Entrepreneur

An entrepreneur is an individual who takes the risk to start their own business based on an idea they have or a product they have created while assuming most of the risks and reaping most of the rewards of the business. Entrepreneurs frequently want change, not only change, they also want to be the bearers of change. They are solution givers and want to interrupt the status . They have a vision like "I want to assemble the world's information" or "I want to put an AC at every desk" and they take an attempt to make this change. In this attempt, some succeed and some fail.

Funders

What is a funder

Funder, sometimes called an angel investor, is a wealthy individual or organization that provides financial backing for jects or business ventures. As a funder, you can offer financial support through a grant, an investment, or a loan.

What's required
  • Financial documents - Basic business plan; cash flow projections; outstanding debtors (i.e. customers who owe you money); up-to-date management accounts (i.e. income statement, balance sheet and cash flow statement); latest annual financial statements; latest VAT statement; last three/six months bank statements, tax clearance certificate.
  • Personal documents - ID documents of owners; marriage certificates of owners (where applicable).
  • Business documents - Company registration documents; office lease or mortgage agreement; shareholder agreements; share register; proof of business address; relevant business licences; accreditations or registrations.
  • Application forms - Prepare your application pack.
  • Research for funding product and plan for investors - Approach the right funder and
  • Pitch - Know your product , practice your pitch and don't deliver the same pitch to everyone adjust your pitch to suit the audience,
